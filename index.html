Our Story

Built by Pet Lovers, for Pet Lovers

Zeta Pet Care Services was founded on one simple belief: every pet deserves to feel loved, even when their owners are away. What began as a small pet-sitting service quickly grew into a full-suite pet care operation, thanks to the trust and loyalty of hundreds of pet families.

Our team brings together trained pet care professionals who are not just employees — they're animal lovers at heart. From our first client to our five-hundredth, our commitment to safety, comfort, and genuine care has never wavered.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, professional pet care that gives owners complete peace of mind and pets a genuinely happy experience.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in pet care — known for excellence, reliability, and a deep love for animals.
